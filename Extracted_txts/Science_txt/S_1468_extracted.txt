8. Sometimes a ligand may be attached through different atoms. Thus
M—NO, is called nitro and M—ONO is called nitrito. Similarly the
SCN group may bond M—SCN thiocyanato or M—NCS isothiocyanato.
These may be named systematically thiocyanato-S or thiocyanato-N to
indicate which atom is bonded to the metal. This convention may be
extended to other cases where the mode of linkage is ambiguous.

9. If any lattice components such as water or solvent of crystallization are
present, these follow the name, and are preceded by the number of
these groups in Arabic numerals.
